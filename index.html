Background

My background started in mechanical engineering. I liked solving physical systems with math, heat transfer, fluid dynamics, the kind of problems where you write an equation and predict reality. Somewhere between my bachelor's in İzmir and my master's in Padova, I realized the systems I actually wanted to build weren't made of metal. They were made of data.

I spent the last nine months at FORE on my MSc thesis, shipping a Python optimization library that now replaces €200,000+ hardware deployments for Italian water networks. In parallel I built SQLOps Guardian, a 4-layer SQL optimization pipeline designed to gracefully degrade when the LLM is unavailable because production systems should never hard-fail on a single dependency. I work end-to-end across the ML lifecycle: data pipelines, model training, evaluation, API deployment, monitoring. Looking for Machine Learning Engineer or AI Engineer roles, remote or EU-based.
